# Geograph Project Transparency Hub

## Executive Summary

A real-time dashboard that eliminates information gaps between Project Management and Customer Success teams, providing complete visibility into project status, team alignment, and client relationships through AI-powered insights and HubSpot integration.

## The Problem We Solve

**Current State:**
- PM and CS teams operate in silos with limited visibility
- Status meetings consume hours weekly to align on basic project updates
- Critical project risks are identified too late
- HubSpot data isn't leveraged for team alignment
- Client satisfaction issues surface after they've escalated

## The Solution

**Project Transparency Hub** provides:
- **Single Source of Truth**: Real-time project status accessible to both teams
- **AI-Powered Insights**: Proactive risk identification and recommendations
- **HubSpot Integration**: Live sync with deal data and client communications
- **Team Alignment**: Clear visibility into PM and CS responsibilities and progress

## Key Business Benefits

### For Leadership (CEO/COO)
- **Portfolio Visibility**: Instant overview of all project health and risks
- **Data-Driven Decisions**: AI insights on project performance and team efficiency
- **Reduced Meeting Overhead**: Eliminates 60% of status alignment meetings

### For Project Management
- **Proactive Risk Management**: AI identifies timeline and resource risks early
- **Client Context**: Visibility into CS team communications and client sentiment
- **Efficient Handoffs**: Clear transition points from sales to delivery

### For Customer Success
- **Project Awareness**: Real-time visibility into project progress and challenges
- **Upsell Identification**: AI highlights expansion opportunities
- **Client Advocacy**: Data to demonstrate value and manage expectations

## How It Works

1. **Connect HubSpot** - Live sync with deals, contacts, and activities
2. **View Project Health** - Color-coded status (Green/Yellow/Red) for all projects
3. **Monitor Team Alignment** - Side-by-side PM and CS task tracking
4. **Get AI Insights** - Ask questions about projects, risks, or team alignment
5. **Track Activities** - Real-time feed of team updates and client communications
